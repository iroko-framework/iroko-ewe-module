## Definition

Controlled vocabulary for high-level biomedical or ethnomedical classification of plant use within the Ew√© module.

---

## Scope note

This scheme captures general categories of reported medicinal application. It does not include preparation methods, dosage, or clinical validation. These values are descriptive and not prescriptive.

---

## Concepts in this scheme

- Digestive support
- Respiratory support
- Skin and topical use
- General tonic / revitalizing
